FEATURE 6A: PRODUCT CONDITION GRADING SYSTEM
Brand New · Refurbished (A+, A, B, C) — Vendor listing, customer filtering, UI display
6A.1 Business Rationale
Refurbished laptops carry significantly higher margin potential for vendors (35-50% margin vs 8-15% on new). Offering graded refurbished alongside new gives Rentfoxxy a wider price band — attracting cost-sensitive buyers (schools, NGOs, SMBs) while retaining enterprise buyers who want Grade A+ or Brand New.

FEATURE 6B: DUAL RATING & REVIEW SYSTEM
Customer rates Vendor (anonymised) · Vendor rates Customer · Trust-based negotiation logic
6B.1 Business Rationale
Two-way ratings create a trust flywheel: high-rated customers get better bid terms from vendors; high-rated anonymous "suppliers" build customer confidence. This is the same principle that powers Alibaba's Gold Supplier ratings and Airbnb's bidirectional reviews. For Rentfoxxy, vendor anonymity is maintained — customers rate their "purchase experience" not a named vendor.

FEATURE 6C: TOKEN PAYMENT & DEFERRED BALANCE SYSTEM
Pay X% to lock stock · Balance due within N hours · Non-refundable token on default · Full payment option
6C.1 Business Rationale
Token-based advance payment is a proven B2B mechanism (similar to advance in real estate, advance booking in manufacturing). It benefits all parties: customer locks in stock and approved bid price; vendor has commitment and cash assurance; Rentfoxxy earns float income on held funds. Research confirms that B2B BNPL and advance-deposit models increase average order value by 20-35% on wholesale platforms (Ankorstore, Hokodo case studies).

6C.3 How Customer Selects Payment Option
Customer selects payment method in the BID FORM (not at checkout). This is part of the bid terms.
In the bid modal (product detail page), after entering quantity and price:
"How would you like to pay if this bid is approved?" — radio selection of 5 options
Each option shows: Token %, Amount preview (calculated live), Balance window, Forfeit warning
Default selected: Full Payment
This selection becomes part of the Bid record. Vendor sees it when reviewing the bid.
Vendor CAN reject a bid based on payment option chosen (e.g., vendor may not want 2% token for large orders).
On bid approval, Razorpay payment link is generated for the TOKEN AMOUNT ONLY (or full if full payment selected).
On token payment: stock is reserved, order created with status STOCK_RESERVED.
Balance payment reminder: automated email + notification at T-12h, T-2h before deadline.
If balance paid on time → order moves to ORDER_PLACED → vendor dispatches.
If balance NOT paid by deadline → order status = TOKEN_FORFEITED, stock released, token kept by Rentfoxxy, small portion forwarded to vendor as compensation (Admin configures split %).

FEATURE 6D: VENDOR PAYMENT METHOD PREFERENCES
Vendor sets accepted payment methods · Customer sees available options before bidding
6D.1 Business Rationale
Different vendors have different operational constraints. A vendor processing ₹50L+ orders may only accept NEFT/RTGS. A smaller vendor may prefer UPI. Displaying accepted payment methods per vendor (anonymised) helps customers plan and reduces bid rejections due to payment method mismatch. This is standard on IndiaMART, Alibaba, and TradeIndia for B2B sellers.
